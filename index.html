New to stokvels?
A stokvel is a South African savings club — 10 to 25 people contributing to a shared pot. Over R50 billion moves through stokvels every year.

Step 5 of 6
Your constitution
Your constitution is ready
Based on the NASASA template, pre-filled from what you entered. This is a legal contract between you and your members.
CONSTITUTION OF
Ubuntu Grocery Stokvel
1. NAME AND PURPOSE
The group shall be known as Ubuntu Grocery Stokvel, a grocery stokvel for the purpose of pooled savings toward annual bulk-buying of groceries for its members.
2. MEMBERSHIP
Membership is by invitation only. The group shall have 12 members. Members must supply ID number, date of birth, and residential address.
3. CONTRIBUTIONS
Each member contributes R 800 monthly. Proof of deposit must be provided within 2 working days.
4. EXECUTIVE ROLES
The group shall appoint a Chairperson, Treasurer, and Secretary. Any withdrawal or constitutional change requires 2-of-3 signatures.
5. MEETINGS
Monthly meetings will be held. At least 50% of members must be present for valid resolutions.
