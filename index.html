3 Revenue Moments for Pay@
MOMENT 1
Group Settlement
Group closes → push notification → member taps → Pay@ checkout appears → payment confirmed. Every settlement = Pay@ transaction fee.
MOMENT 2
In-App Partner Purchase
Order via 60|60 inside GroupPay → Pay@ checkout to complete → auto-logged as group expense. Zero friction, double revenue.
MOMENT 3
QR & PayShap Request
Scan to pay at a restaurant or show a QR code for members to scan and pay their share. PayShap RTP notification also triggered instantly.
